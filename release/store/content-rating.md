# Store Content Rating Notes

This file records the content rating inputs that must be reflected in Google Play Console and App Store Connect. It is not proof that either console questionnaire has been submitted.

## Rating Inputs

- Category: Finance.
- Target audience: adult and general Korean mobile users; not directed to children.
- User-generated content: community posts and comments are supported and require reporting/moderation.
- Ads: contextual ads or partner placements may appear and must be labeled.
- External links: privacy policy, support, and safe service links may open outside the app.
- Gambling, contests, real-money prizes, loan approval, investment returns, and health outcome guarantees: not provided.
- Financial institution or brokerage service: not provided.
- Final age and content rating must be confirmed in Google Play Console and App Store Connect before public rollout.

## Reviewer Notes

- Community moderation, report handling, and privacy controls must be active before public launch.
- Store questionnaires must be rechecked if ads, partner content, community behavior, or external links change.
