# Store Review Notes

This file is the repository-local draft for App Store and Google Play review notes. It is not a credential store. Passwords, one-time codes, recovery codes, API keys, tokens, and private links must be provided only through the relevant store console or another approved secure channel.

## Reviewer Access

- Reviewer account email: reviewer@salaryhijacking.com
- Reviewer password: provide out-of-band through the store console only; do not commit it.
- Test data: seeded demo payroll, budget, expense, savings, LV UP, notification, and community records only.
- Account state: reviewer account should already have onboarding complete and sample data loaded.

## Review Path

- Log in with the reviewer account.
- Confirm salary home, daily budget, plan, notifications, LV UP, community, and profile tabs.
- Confirm profile privacy export and withdrawal request surfaces are reachable.
- All displayed financial values are sample data.
- Ads and partner content are contextual-only and clearly labeled when visible.
- Account deletion support is available through profile withdrawal request and support contact.

## Review Safety Notes

- The app does not provide banking, loan approval, investment advice, insurance, gambling, real-money prizes, or health outcome guarantees.
- The app uses server-authoritative payroll, budget, expense, savings, and hijack amount calculations.
- Production review remains blocked until the deployed API, database, push service, and EAS store builds are verified.
