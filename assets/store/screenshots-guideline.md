# Store Screenshot Guideline

Store images must present 급여납치 as a Finance app for Korean mobile users. The visual message should be practical and app-first: paycheck planning, daily budget control, reminders, LV UP routines, and community support.

## Required Set

- Minimum phone screenshots: 5
- Recommended phone screenshots: 8
- Google Play feature graphic: `feature_graphic_google_play.png`
- Optional app preview video: `app_preview_15s.mp4`

## Quality Gates

- Use Korean copy, `ko-KR` locale, and sample data only.
- Match actual mobile screens and available features.
- Keep ads or partner content labeled when visible.
- Do not show raw salary, expense, savings, account, card, loan, phone, email, token, or raw device identifiers.
- Do not promise profit, debt relief, investment return, guaranteed savings, or health outcomes.
- Export PNG images suitable for store upload.

- `01_home_salary.png`
- `02_daily_budget.png`
- `03_plan_setting.png`
- `04_notifications.png`
- `05_level_up.png`
- `feature_graphic_google_play.png`
